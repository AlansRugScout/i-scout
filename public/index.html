Questions & answers

Everything you need to know

How accurate are the valuations? +

Very good — within a realistic market range. 3scouts uses Claude Advanced Vision, Anthropic's most capable image analysis model, cross-referenced against auction records and current market data. Valuations are expressed as a range (e.g. €800–€1,200) rather than a single figure, which is how professional appraisers work. The range reflects genuine market variation based on condition, provenance and buyer. Without physically examining an item, no appraisal can be absolute — but our assessments are consistently comparable to what you'd receive from a specialist dealer.

Can it really identify fakes? +

Better than most. 3scouts analyses construction methods, materials, hallmarks, signatures, patina, and stylistic consistency against documented examples. It gives you an authenticity confidence percentage and flags specific red flags to verify in person. It won't replace physical examination by a specialist for high-value pieces — but it will tell you whether something is worth that examination, and what to look for when you get there.

What kinds of items work best? +

Anything with visual identifying features — hallmarks, signatures, maker's marks, stylistic period indicators, or distinctive construction. Antique silver, ceramics, art, maps, coins, jewellery, watches, vintage collectables and sporting memorabilia all work exceptionally well. The more detail you provide in your description — and the better your photos — the more precise your report will be. That said, 3scouts has successfully appraised everything from Georgian rattles to modern earbuds.

How is this different from a Google search? +

A Google search returns general information. 3scouts returns a specific professional assessment of your specific item — authenticity confidence, component-by-component condition grading, comparable recent sales, a fair value range, and a plain-English recommendation. It also watches eBay continuously on your behalf, alerting you the moment something matching your brief appears — something no search engine does. Think of it as having an expert on call, rather than a library.

Is the “Value an Item” free valuation really free? +

Completely free. No card, no trial, no catch. Submit your item, receive your full Deep Analysis report by email, and decide for yourself whether 3scouts is right for you. If you'd like more valuations or want Scout One watching eBay on your behalf, that's when a subscription starts — with a 30-day free trial and no charge until day 31.

How quickly will I receive my report? +

Usually within the hour — often within minutes. Your report arrives by email as soon as the analysis is complete, with a link to your full branded report page. Subscribers also receive a push notification via the 3scouts app the moment it's ready.

What if I disagree with the valuation? +

That's entirely reasonable — valuations involve judgement, and physical examination can reveal details photographs can't. If you believe the assessment is significantly off, email alan@3scouts.com with your reasoning. We take feedback seriously and use it to continuously improve. We'd also always recommend verifying high-value finds with a specialist before purchasing or selling.

Can I cancel my subscription easily? +

One click, any time, no questions asked. Manage your subscription at any time through your account — there are no cancellation fees, no notice periods, and no awkward phone calls. We believe if 3scouts isn't delivering value, you shouldn't be paying for it.

Something not covered here? Email us at alan@3scouts.com — we respond to every message personally, usually the same day.

Important notice

Without physically seeing and examining an item, no definitive appraisal or purchase recommendation can be made. All 3scouts valuations, authenticity assessments and condition reports are guidance based on available listing photographs and market data only. They are provided to assist your research and decision-making — not as a substitute for expert physical examination. In most circumstances you are protected by eBay's Money Back Guarantee — if an item is not as described, eBay's Buyer Protection policy generally entitles you to a full refund. 3scouts accepts no liability for purchasing decisions made on the basis of its assessments.
