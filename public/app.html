Add 3scouts to your Home Screen

One-tap appraisals from your home screen — follow these steps:

1
Tap the Share button
At the bottom of Safari, tap the Share button — it looks like a box with an arrow pointing up ⬆
⬆
2
Tap "Add to Home Screen"
Scroll down in the Share menu and tap "Add to Home Screen"
📱
⚠️ Important: On the next screen you'll see a toggle that says "Open as Web Page" — make sure this is turned OFF (grey, not green). If it's on, the app won't work properly.
3
Tap "Add"
Tap Add in the top right corner. The 3scouts icon will appear on your home screen.
✓
4
Open from your home screen
Tap the 3scouts icon on your home screen — you're in, no login needed every time.
🏠

Android? Tap the three dots menu ⋮ in Chrome → "Add to Home Screen".
No "Open as Web Page" toggle on Android.
