One point to verify

The seller has not photographed the movement from the rear. Before purchasing, request a photo of the back plate showing the full engraving and balance cock — this is where Dent's signature finishing is most visible and most easily distinguished from lesser makers with similar outward cases. This is a standard request and any legitimate seller will accommodate it.
