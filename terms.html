Section 04

Standard alerts & Deep Analysis

Your subscription includes two levels of service:

  • Standard Alert: An automated email notification when a potential match is identified, including the listing image, asking price, and a brief AI assessment. Included free with all plans on an unlimited basis.
  • Deep Analysis: A full professional appraisal report including authenticity assessment, condition grading, comparable sales data, and a detailed buying recommendation. Deep Analyses are drawn from your monthly allowance. Additional Deep Analyses may be purchased at €2 per 10.

You will be notified when your Deep Analysis allowance is running low. Your Scout will never exceed your monthly budget without your explicit approval.

All i-Scout valuations, authenticity assessments, and condition reports are AI-assisted guidance based on available listing photographs and market data only. They are provided to assist your research and decision-making — not as a substitute for expert physical examination."

i-Scout appraisals are estimates based on available photographic evidence and comparable sales data. They do not constitute professional valuations, certified authentications, or guarantees of any kind. i-Scout strongly recommends independent expert verification before making any significant purchase.

i-Scout is not responsible for purchasing decisions made on the basis of its alerts or appraisals. The responsibility for any purchase decision rests entirely with the subscriber.
